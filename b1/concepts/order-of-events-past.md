## What this helps you do

Make clear what happened first and what happened next in the past.

```{practice}

```

## When to use it

- Tell stories and report processes.
- Explain steps or instructions.

```{practice}
Create 3 short situations with one model sentence each using: 1) vorher/danach 2) bevor 3) nachdem + Plusquamperfekt.
```

## Key phrases

| German | English | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| vorher/danach | before/after (then) | adverbs |
| bevor | before | subclause, verb at end |
| nachdem | after | often with Plusquamperfekt |
| vor/nach + Dativ | before/after | preposition + noun |

---

## Examples

- **Vorher** frühstücke ich, **danach** fahre ich zur Arbeit.  
  _Before that I have breakfast, after that I go to work._
- Ich frühstücke, **bevor** ich zur Arbeit fahre.  
  _I have breakfast before I go to work._
- **Nachdem** ich gefrühstückt hatte, fuhr ich zur Arbeit.  
  _After I had had breakfast, I went to work._
- Ich frühstücke **vor** der Arbeit.  
  _I eat breakfast before work._

## Register and tone

Use Plusquamperfekt to mark the earlier of two past actions when needed.

## Mini practice
